OUR STORY

From Passion to Perfection

Roast & Rich began in 2018 when two lifelong coffee enthusiasts traveled through coffee-growing regions searching for the perfect cup.

What started as weekend experiments with hand-roasted beans soon became a mission: to bring exceptional artisan coffee to people who appreciate quality, craftsmanship, and authenticity.

Today, Roast & Rich partners directly with ethical coffee farmers and carefully roasts every batch to highlight the unique character of each bean. We believe coffee is more than a drink—it's a ritual, a conversation, and a moment of connection.
